HISTORY AND AIM

Zhongshan Sanxiang Shunyu Paper Products Company was established in January 2008. Our factory is a new type venture of energy-saving and emission-reducing with eco-friendly idea and with environmental protection as our sole objective.


PRODUCT FEATURES

Our company is specialized in producing molded paper pulp packing products. With recycled paper as raw material, the paper pulp is processed into ready-use pulp material which is then molded into required shape in special molding machine by method of vacuum absorption. The molded pieces will be turned into strongly inner supported products through the drying facilities. The products can be made in different shapes and can be used in various ways. Our products do not contain harmful chemicals. They are no smells and naturally resolvable. With good shock-absorbing performance, they do not generate static electricity, do not become deformed after molding and can be 100% recyclable. Our products can be used widely in these areas : Electronics and electric appliances packaging, industrial and agricultural products packaging, Home necessities packaging, toys packaging, shoe inserts packaging, craft items packaging, bulky electric appliances unit packaging etc.
