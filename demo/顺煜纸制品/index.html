Company Brief Introduction


ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION ENTERPRISE

Zhongshan Sanxiang Shunyu Paper Products Company was established in January 2008. Our factory is a new type venture of energy-saving and emission-reducing with eco-friendly idea and with environmental protection as our sole objective.

And our products can be used widely in these areas : Electronics and electric appliances packaging, industrial and agricultural products packaging, Home necessities packaging, toys packaging, shoe inserts packaging, craft items packaging, bulky electric appliances unit packaging etc.
